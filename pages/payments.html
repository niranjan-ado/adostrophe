Payments and Billing

Transparent Pricing for All Our Digital Marketing & Immersive Services

At Adostrophe, we believe in complete transparency and clarity when it comes to our pricing and billing. All payments for services such as Matterport 3D scanning, Google Ads management, web development, and others are processed securely through Zoho Books.

View Pricing Details

Our Commitment to Transparent Pricing

Besides hosting fees for certain services, we do not have any other recurring charges. As trusted service providers of renowned brands, it is our duty to be transparent about our pricing upfront, which is detailed below.

Order Specifications

Typically, we handle large orders to ensure maximum impact for our clients. For instance, we frequently manage Matterport scans for properties over 10,000 square feet and Google Ads campaigns with a monthly ad spend exceeding ₹3,00,000.

However, we also consider smaller projects on a case-by-case basis, especially if there is potential for ongoing partnerships or referrals. Our goal is to find a solution that fits your needs.

Matterport Pricing

  • Payment for Matterport 3D scanning services is required upfront.
  • Our standard rates start at ₹10 per square foot.
  • The minimum payment for any Matterport project is ₹10,000, which covers scanning services for properties up to 1,000 square feet.
  • Lifetime Free Edits: All edits, such as Mattertags™, Labels, and Highlight Reels, are available for the lifetime of your Matterport tour at no extra charge.

Google Ads Pricing

  • New Search Campaign Setup: Costs $100. This is payable after the detailed spreadsheet (for Google Ads Editor) is provided for your review and approval.
  • Nonprofit Google Ads Setup: A subsidized rate of $50 for nonprofit organizations.
  • Ongoing Monthly Management: Fees are capped at a flat 15% of your monthly ad spend (the amount directly paid to Google for clicks).
  • Nonprofit Management: A reduced rate of 10% of monthly ad spend for nonprofit organizations. These fees may be further reduced or entirely waived in certain cases, based on the organization's mission and impact (refer to our Nonprofit Services page for details).
  • For nonprofits, the setup of a Google Ad Grant account costs $50, and the management fee is 10% per month. These fees may be reduced or waived in certain cases.
