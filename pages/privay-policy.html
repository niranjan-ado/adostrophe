Privacy Policy

Your Data Privacy Matters to Adostrophe

This Privacy Policy outlines how Adostrophe operates with respect to user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect & Why

At Adostrophe, we prioritize your privacy. We want to be absolutely clear about what information we collect and, more importantly, what we *do not*.

No General Customer Information Collection

Adostrophe does NOT collect or store general personal customer information from website visitors or inquiries beyond what is necessary to respond to your direct communications (e.g., an email address if you send us an email). We do not use web analytics that personally identifies you, nor do we track your browsing habits across our site for marketing purposes.

Our website is designed primarily as an informational showcase of our services. We believe in minimal data footprint.

Information for Active Projects

We only collect and store information strictly necessary for those who have **officially engaged with us on a project or service.** This includes:

  • Contact Details: Names, company/organization name, email addresses, phone numbers required for communication and invoicing.
  • Project-Specific Data: Information directly related to the service being provided (e.g., property addresses for Matterport scans, Google Ads account IDs for management, website access credentials for web development). This data is used solely for project execution and delivery.
  • Billing Information: Data required for secure payment processing via Zoho Books (e.g., billing address), but sensitive payment card details are handled directly by secure third-party payment processors (PayPal, Stripe etc.) and are NOT stored by Adostrophe.

This information is collected on a consent basis as part of our client agreements and is used exclusively for fulfilling our contractual obligations.

How We Use and Share Information

The limited information we collect from active clients is used solely for operational purposes related to our services.

Service Delivery

We use your contact and project-specific information to deliver the services you've contracted us for (e.g., scheduling scans, managing ad campaigns, developing websites).

Communication

To communicate with you about your projects, invoices, updates, and any support inquiries you may have.

Third-Party Sharing (Strictly Limited)

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Information is only shared with third-party service providers (e.g., Matterport for tour processing, Google Ads for campaign management, Zoho Books for invoicing, secure payment gateways) strictly when necessary for the provision of our services, and under agreements that ensure their commitment to data privacy.

For example, if you engage us for a Matterport tour, the necessary property data is uploaded to Matterport's secure platform for processing. Your Google Ads account details are used within the Google Ads platform for campaign management.

Data Security & Retention

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we hold against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Security Measures

We utilize industry-standard security protocols and practices for any data we store, including secure servers and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain client information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Once your project is concluded and all financial obligations are met, data not required for ongoing service or legal compliance is securely deleted.

Cookies & Third-Party Links

Our website uses minimal cookies and may contain links to external sites.

Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies necessary for basic site functionality (e.g., session cookies). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies that collect personal data without your explicit consent. You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may affect site functionality.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by us (e.g., Matterport, Google, social media platforms). Please be aware that we have no control over the content and practices of these sites, and cannot accept responsibility or liability for their respective privacy policies.

Your Privacy Rights

As our client, you have certain rights regarding the personal information we hold related to your projects.

Access & Correction

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections of any inaccuracies.

Deletion & Restriction

You may also have the right to request the deletion of your personal information or to restrict its processing, subject to our legal and contractual obligations.

Contact Us for Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Adostrophe may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will post any changes on this page.
