Matterport Virtual Tours for Showrooms in India

With an example in Mumbai, we illustrate how Matterport virtual tours for showrooms are unique ideas, for their digital marketing efforts.

By Adostrophe | Oct 12, 2023 | AEC, Matterport, Retail, Showrooms

The showroom is a critical component of any business, as it represents the customer-facing side of operations. Matterport virtual tours offer a realistic portrayal of showrooms and customer experience centers, promoting products effectively.

Google Street View Integration

Matterport tours can be viewed in Google Maps at a resolution of 135 megapixels. Customers can pan around in 360 degrees and click to move to the next scan point. Clickable chevrons (white arrows) indicate these interactive areas.

Professional scanners, such as the Matterport Pro 2, Pro 3, and Leica BLK G1, capture high-definition imagery with accurate distance measurement. Unlike consumer 360-degree cameras like the Ricoh Theta or Insta360 devices, these professional scanners ensure precise measurements. As a Matterport service provider, we use only professional scanners for all our projects.

Follow us on Instagram to see some of the sites we’ve captured and subscribe to our YouTube channel for updates on new videos. Our channel features highlight reels of the Matterport virtual tours we create.

4K Videos

Along with the Matterport URL, we provide 2D images and a 4K video as part of our package. Traditional videos don’t offer the same level of interactivity; however, in a Matterport virtual tour, viewers can navigate and shop as they prefer. For example, in the video of a Victorinox watch showroom, viewers can click to move around and explore.

This next video is of a furniture showroom in Bhubaneswar. A customer could see the wide range of items for sale, but only in a Matterport virtual tour would she be able to take measurements. In the architecture and interior decor industry, distance measurement is an important feature.

If the customer can measure the length and width of a dining table, there is literally no need for a physical visit to the showroom. It saves a lot of time and effort, and reduces carbon emissions.

2D Images

Unlike traditional photography with a DSLR or mirrorless camera, 3D virtual tours enable us to extract 2D images from multiple angles during post-processing. These images can be used for social media or print collateral.

The versatile virtual tour allows the extraction of 2D images for use in Photoshop and other Adobe apps to create marketing materials. While the Matterport virtual tour itself cannot be edited, this ensures authenticity and sets clear expectations for the customer.

Getting Started

Contact us for a personalized consultation, and we’ll schedule a scan of your showroom, typically outside of business hours—usually an hour or two before opening.

In our email, we’ll provide more details about our offerings and how you can prepare your showroom. Our experience ensures high-quality deliverables at an affordable cost, starting at ₹10 per square foot. We don’t charge extra for locations within India.
