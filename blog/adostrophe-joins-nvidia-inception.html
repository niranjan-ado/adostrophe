Adostrophe Joins NVIDIA Inception

Adostrophe announces its inclusion in NVIDIA Inception, accelerating innovation in immersive tech and an upcoming mobile gaming app.

By Niranjan | June 03, 2025 | NVIDIA

Adostrophe has been accepted into NVIDIA Inception, a program supporting companies that are developing advanced technological solutions across various industries.

Currently, Adostrophe’s primary focus is on Matterport virtual tours and 360° photography. For future initiatives, we’re building out our full-stack web application capabilities and developing a mobile gaming app, Finostrophe, both of which will incorporate machine learning to deliver digital experiences.

Participation in NVIDIA Inception will facilitate accelerated development of these upcoming machine learning applications, including sophisticated deep learning models for Finostrophe, and will also enhance our immersive experiences. This will be made possible through the program’s access to robust computing resources and specialized technical guidance.

Adostrophe’s Digital Journey

Adostrophe commenced operations in 2017, initially providing strategic search marketing and Google Ads services. Our service scope subsequently grew to encompass Matterport virtual tours and 360° photography. As a participant in the Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub, we leverage a resilient cloud infrastructure to construct solutions that are both scalable and secure.

We’re currently broadening our skill set to incorporate full-stack web application development and SEO. This development work utilizes C#, Python, JavaScript, and Figma for user interface design, adhering to web.dev guidelines for performance, accessibility, and search engine optimization. Our development practices also integrate advanced AI tools like Copilot for code generation and Gemini for deeper insights into digital solutions. Adostrophe also provides pro bono Google Ad Grant management for non-profit organizations, aiming to maximize their digital outreach without financial burden.

Finostrophe: Cultivating Financial Acumen with Machine Learning

Finostrophe is an upcoming mobile gaming application from Adostrophe, created to improve financial understanding among India’s younger generation. Designed as a fun and engaging interactive game, Finostrophe aims to be a daily motivator for users to manage their spending and cultivate habits that avoid debt. Through its gameplay, and by leveraging machine learning—including sophisticated deep learning techniques for analyzing a user’s financial behaviors—it will offer personalized, data-driven insights and guidance, helping them progress toward their financial goals. Beyond this core interactive experience, Finostrophe will also suggest modules on topics like entrepreneurship, insurance, and cybersecurity, providing valuable information sourced from authoritative and government bodies to support their comprehensive learning journey.

Adostrophe is building Finostrophe with accessibility in mind. It’s designed for use by individuals in rural India, those with older mobile devices, and users with varying literacy levels, performing effectively even on hardware with limited resources and inconsistent internet. To make financial education truly relatable, Finostrophe incorporates ancestral data, Matterport, and Google Street View APIs, transitioning real-world financial contexts into an interactive digital setting. Plans for future versions include Apple Vision Pro AR compatibility.

Adostrophe’s development process for Finostrophe follows agile methodologies and continuously incorporates user feedback. The initial launch will cater to Indian users with Indian locales; however, the company plans to open-source the project on GitHub for adaptation and use in other countries.

NVIDIA Inception: Our New Tech Playground

Adostrophe’s involvement in NVIDIA Inception grants access to computational assets and technical guidance. This support will be instrumental in creating our machine learning applications, including advanced deep learning models, and in improving our immersive experiences.

This association will also facilitate significant enhancements to Adostrophe’s current Matterport virtual tour services. We’re planning improvements such as custom tours with increased accessibility for visually impaired users (e.g., audio description scripts and tailored keyboard shortcuts), refined spatial mapping, voice-guided navigation, and enhanced security features through ML-driven optimization. Our work in real-time rendering is also progressing, aiming to support high-quality visual representation of interactive environments for all our immersive initiatives. These efforts involve exploring extended reality concepts, the mathematical foundations of computer graphics, and advanced 3D JavaScript libraries (like Three.js or Babylon.js) for building complex, interactive web-based environments.

A key focus area for us involves deploying secure machine learning systems to protect sensitive financial information, building upon our robust cloud security protocols. Our ongoing integration of cloud-based cognitive services will continue to bolster accessibility and security across all our digital platforms.

A Vision for Accessible Modern Tech

“NVIDIA was always an exciting word in the world of gaming, and we’re happy to get the green light from them for our initiatives. We hope this support will provide valuable technical resources that might enable us to build practical, modern tech solutions for businesses, non-profits, and the general public.”

Niranjan, Director at Adostrophe

NVIDIA Inception supports startups through crucial stages of product creation, from initial prototyping to operational deployment. Each Inception participant receives a tailored set of continuous benefits, including credits for the NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute, preferential rates on NVIDIA hardware and software, and direct technological guidance. These provisions furnish startups with the foundational tools necessary for expansion and innovation.
