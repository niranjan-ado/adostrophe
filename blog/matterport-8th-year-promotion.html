Matterport for Real Estate in India: A Growing Trend

Adostrophe continues to provide Google Ads and Matterport virtual tour scanning services across India. Our recent projects include a residential real estate property in Powai, Mumbai, showcasing the growing adoption of digital marketing in the sector.

The real estate sector in India is booming, employing around 70 million people and projected to reach a valuation of **one trillion USD by 2030**, according to Statista. Developers and homeowners are increasingly recognizing the power of virtual tours to expedite property sales and rentals.

In bustling cities like Mumbai and Bengaluru, where even short distances can lead to significant traffic delays, Matterport 3D virtual tours offer an invaluable solution. They can be accessed from any device, including smartphones, allowing potential buyers or tenants to explore different parts of a property without ever leaving their current location. Unlike traditional images and videos, each virtual tour is provided via a unique, shareable URL.

---

Environmental Impact and Competitive Edge

According to Matterport’s ESG report dated December 11, 2023, over **2.5 million virtual tours were created in 2022**, contributing to the avoidance of **382,640 tonnes of carbon emissions globally**. India's potential for virtual tour adoption is substantial, aligning with the increasing embrace of digital marketing. A construction company that features a virtual tour on its website can gain a significant competitive advantage, encouraging others to follow suit.

Beyond residential properties, virtual tours are widely utilized in **commercial real estate**, including office spaces. In architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC), these tours aid in precise distance measurement, greatly enhancing coordination among remote teams.

---

The Matterport Advantage

Matterport uses specialized cameras (scanners) equipped with Infrared and LiDAR technology to accurately measure indoor distances. While basic virtual tours can be created with smartphones or 360-degree cameras, professional Matterport scanners are recommended for **superior image quality and precise distance measurement**. Public virtual tours feature panoramic images in **135 MP resolution** and can be seamlessly uploaded to **Google Street View**, enabling users to navigate properties directly within Google Maps.

Matterport holds a dominant position in the virtual tour market, much like Google in search engines. To further boost the Indian economy, businesses can create virtual tours of their properties and promote them effectively through **Google Ads**. For example, a business can display its website listing as a sponsored ad when users search for "3bhk house for sale in Mumbai," driving targeted traffic.
